Pornstar Martini Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 50ml vanilla vodka
  • 20ml passion fruit puree
  • 20ml lime juice
  • 10ml vanilla syrup
  • Prosecco (to serve on the side)
  • Ice cubes

Execution

  • Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
  • Add vanilla vodka, passion fruit puree, lime juice, and vanilla syrup.
  • Shake vigorously for 10-15 seconds until well chilled.
  • Strain the mixture into a chilled martini glass.
  • Garnish with half a passion fruit on top.
  • Serve with a shot of Prosecco on the side in a separate glass.

Key Tips

  • For an extra burst of flavor, muddle some fresh passion fruit before adding to the shaker.
  • Serve the Prosecco chilled, but don't mix it into the cocktailâ€”keep it on the side for a perfect sip alongside.
  • Adjust the sweetness by adding more or less vanilla syrup based on your taste preference.
