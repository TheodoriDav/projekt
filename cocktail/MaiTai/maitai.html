Mai Tai Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 30ml white rum
  • 30ml dark rum
  • 15ml lime juice
  • 15ml orange liqueur (like Cointreau)
  • 15ml orgeat syrup
  • 10ml grenadine
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint sprig (for garnish)

Execution

  • Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
  • Pour in the white rum, dark rum, lime juice, orange liqueur, orgeat syrup, and grenadine.
  • Shake well for 10-15 seconds until the ingredients are well mixed and chilled.
  • Strain the mixture into a glass filled with crushed ice.
  • Float the dark rum on top by pouring it slowly over the back of a spoon.
  • Garnish with a mint sprig or a lime wheel.

Key Tips

  • For a more authentic taste, use a high-quality orgeat syrup made with almonds.
  • Ensure to float the dark rum carefully on top to create the layered effect.
  • If you prefer a sweeter drink, adjust the amount of grenadine to taste.
