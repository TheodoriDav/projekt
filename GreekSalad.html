Greek Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes - 2, medium-sized
  • Cucumber - 1, peeled and sliced
  • Red onion - 1/2, thinly sliced
  • Green bell pepper - 1, sliced
  • Black olives - 1/2 cup
  • Feta cheese - 200g, cubed or in blocks
  • Extra virgin olive oil - 3 tbsp
  • Red wine vinegar - 1 tbsp
  • Oregano - 1 tsp, dried
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

Execution:

  1. Start by washing and cutting the tomatoes into wedges.

  2. Peel and slice the cucumber, then cut into half-moon shapes.

  3. Thinly slice the red onion and bell pepper, then add to the salad bowl.

  4. Carefully add the olives and feta cheese (either cubes or a block) on top of the vegetables.

  5. Drizzle the olive oil and red wine vinegar over the salad.

  6. Sprinkle dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste, and toss gently to combine.

  7. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Key Tips:

  • Use high-quality extra virgin olive oil for the best flavor.
  • For added crunch, try adding some capers or a handful of fresh herbs like parsley or mint.
  • Let the salad sit for a few minutes before serving to allow the flavors to blend and marinate.
